Here you have a data visualization of the structure of different departments of\u00a0the\u00a0Generalitat de Catalunya, based on data on their website. Our seismograph<\/a> is based on this information.<\/p>\n

The map of Economy and Knowledge<\/a>\u00a0(enigma\u00a0solved here<\/a>).<\/p>\n

You may open and close each department unit and see how many depend on it by clicking on the circles on the left of each name.<\/p>\n

A yellow circle allows to unfold units to the right and to fold them up to the left. Blue circles may only unfold . In order not to get lost, the last circle you clicked on turns yellow.<\/p>\n

The circles become larger or smaller depending on the number of units that depend on them.<\/p>\n

At the top left there’s a text search box to look for units. At the bottom left there’s a box with the name of the unit and how many depend on it, its code number and the name of the person responsible. A click on the code number takes you to the corresponding page on the Generalitat website.<\/p>\n

We invite you to explore!<\/p>\n

Our\u00a0visualizations of Departaments of the Catalan government la Generalitat derive from an\u00a0XML file found on the open data portal of Generalitat\u00a0o<\/a>n<\/span>\u00a026 April 2013. There is a copy\u00a0here<\/a>. The hierarchy between the different organization within each Departament was added by scraping. The\u00a0code<\/a>\u00a0and data (csv<\/a>\u00a0or\u00a0json<\/a>, produced in\u00a0python<\/a>) are available at\u00a0GitHub. All collaboration and feedback is very welcome.<\/span><\/p>\n
